Mustang(old school car) and some facts about them

1. Mustang is a famous American muscle car that was first introduced by Ford in 1964 as a 2-door coupe and convertible, and became an icon of the 1960s.

2.It was inspired by European sports cars, but was designed to be affordable and accessible for Americans.

3. Mustangs had a range of engines, from a basic 6-cylinder to a powerful 8-cylinder engine that produced up to 271 horsepower.

4. Mustangs were popular for racing and performance and appeared in movies like Bullitt and Gone in 60 Seconds.

5. Mustangs underwent several design changes over the years, including a significant one in 1971 when they became larger and more luxurious.

6. Classic Mustangs are highly desired by collectors and enthusiasts, and are often restored and modified for performance or aesthetic reasons.

7. Other classic cars from the same era include the Chevrolet Camaro, Pontiac Firebird, and Dodge Challenger.
